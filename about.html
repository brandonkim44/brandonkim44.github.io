a little bit about me



my background

Born and raised in Northern Virginia, I am a 23-year old recent grad from the University of Virginia (wahoowa). Following graduation, I worked at a fintech startup in Philadelphia, PA, as an operations specialist. After learning and growing a ton in Philly, I returned home to Virginia and enrolled in a 16-week full stack development course to explore my interest in web development and software engineering.


I learn quickly. I think big. And I enjoy helping others.

my interests

Although I'm still figuring out what my tech interests are, I'm currently fascinated by front-end engineering due to its impact on users' experience, its constantly changing and improving front-end technologies, and the creativity involved in turning ideas into pixel-perfect views.


In my free time, I love running and finding hidden gems in nature, experimenting with new recipes, writing short stories and poems, and playing board games with friends and family.
